# L109 Mercenary Progression Adventure Lane

## Decision

The first genre-specific plotline lane is adult guild/mercenary mission
progression adventure fantasy, captured in
`docs/method-packs/mercenary-progression-adventure-v0.md`.

## Rationale

The harness now has enough stable quality telemetry to stop mining drafting
arms as the main activity. The next product risk is story shape: avoiding vague
fantasy soup while creating a repeatable commercial series engine.

A guild or mercenary job loop is the simplest first engine because it gives
each novella a clear contract, arena, tactical complication, progression
payoff, relationship shift, faction consequence, and next hook. Dungeon/ruin
exploration stays available as a mission type inside that loop.

## Implications

- Work on the next lane should produce a Book 1 contract packet and plotline
  shape before broad drafting.
- `--quality-telemetry-packet` remains the evidence surface for drafting
  checks, not a gate.
- Do not reopen generic method-pack expansion, POC branches, harem positioning,
  academy scaffolds, or base-building continuity until the first mission loop
  proves useful.
